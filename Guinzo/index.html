Projects

Explore Guinzo’s tailored solutions designed to enhance efficiency and drive growth for your business.

ProMatch

Promatch is a solution that uses NLP techniques to analyze CVs submitted by candidates and assign a match rate based on the needs and skills required for the job offer.

IFRS 17 Compliance and ALM Modeling

The platform includes models for calculating key metrics such as the Contractual Service Margin and Risk Adjustment, along with comprehensive Asset Liability Management models to optimize asset-liability matching and mitigate financial risks.

KYC Platform for Financial Institutions

Solutions designed to streamline the Know Your Customer (KYC) process, utilizing advanced machine learning pipelines to enhance search accuracy and reduce false positives.

AML System for Financial Institutions

An Anti-Money Laundering system designed to detect suspicious transactions related to money laundering and terrorist financing. The project employs predefined rules and AI techniques to identify and flag potential risks.

Diffusion Art Generator

AI-powered project that uses advanced diffusion models to generate high-quality images from textual prompts. The system interprets the descriptive language and iteratively refines random noise into detailed visual art.
